Scheduling Payments

Dates

There are two important dates in the bill payment process.

The "Payment Due Date"

This is the last date on which you want Collections Marketing Center, LLC to receive your payment. This date must be one of our Business Days. In many instances, this is probably the payment due date noted on your billing statement. If the balance you owe Collections Marketing Center, LLC bears daily interest, however, you may want to select an earlier date.

The "Processing Date"

This is the date on which you tell us that funds should be credited to your Collections Marketing Center, LLC account and is also the date on which funds may be drawn or deducted from your Funding Account (funds may be drawn or deducted from your Funding Account either on the Processing Date or within the next two business days).

All payments on your Collections Marketing Center, LLC account made within the Collections Marketing Center, LLC Service website must follow the following rule: all payments made on your Collections Marketing Center, LLC account either within the Collections Marketing Center, LLC Service website or through other payment methods to Collections Marketing Center, LLC may not exceed the then current account balance. If a payment scheduled on the Collections Marketing Center, LLC Service website will cause any of these rules to be broken, Collections Marketing Center, LLC in its discretion reserves the right to either modify or cancel the payment if you fail to follow the rules.
